R Tutorial

About This Page

This page contains some resources for getting you started with R/RStudio. R is an open-source programming language for statistical computing. It is one of the most popular tools among scientists for processing, analyzing, and visualizing their data. One of the most powerful features of R is the ability to download and use "packages" - essentially, collections of functions that have been written by other researchers/programmers to perform specific tasks.

This page is divided into different parts for convenience. The first part introduces the concepts of data types, variables, and data structures. The second part introduces functions, conditional statements, and downloading/using R packages. All of this material will be covered during labs in class.
